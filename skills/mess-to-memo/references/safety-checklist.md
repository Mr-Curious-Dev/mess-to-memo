# Safety Checklist

Before delivery or Notion publication, check for:

- leaked tokens;
- passwords;
- credentials;
- hashes;
- phone numbers;
- emails;
- personal data;
- Telegram message IDs in user-facing outputs;
- unsupported claims;
- duplicated items;
- weak source references;
- broken links;
- category mistakes;
- unresolved conflicts that should be marked for review.
